Majorette

What is Majorette?

Derived from the Dutch word"Dansmariekes," the original majorettes were carnival dancers who twirled balloons in their performances. When the style reached the American South, it morphed into what HBCUs Know today.

Who invented this form of dance?

Majorette originated as a carnival dance form Rhineland Germany, where young women who perform this dance were the highlight of the show.
